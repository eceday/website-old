What is ECE Day?

ECE Day is a day dedicated to celebrating the spirit of Electrical and Computer Engineering and bringing awareness of all that it offers to its students, faculty, and beloved alumni. Throughout the day, there will be many opportunities for students to learn virtually about the numerous exciting fields within ECE and the joys of being a part of the ECE family!

  More about the platform: gather.town

Gather.town is a software platform that tries to recreate the feelings of going to an event in person. The interactions include video streaming to peers near you, broadcast a video stream to entire rooms of participants, and interacting with special items that allow remote interaction with virtual games and websites.

  Frequently Asked Questions
What time is ECE Day?
ECE Day is taking place on April 7th. The event starts at noon and activities extend throughout the evening.
Who can attend ECE Day?
Everyone who is interested in ECE is encouraged to attend! You do not have to be a current or future ECE student!
How long are the workshops?
The workshops will be roughly an hour long. We will have three workshops that start at 12pm, 1pm, and 2pm!
Is this a recruiting event?
This is not explicitly a recruiting event. However, individual companies might be accepting resumes so it could be a good idea to have that resume at the ready.
How much will it cost?
ECE Day is completely free for all students to attend!
