  What is ECE Day?

ECE Day is a celebration of everything that encompasses the spirit of Electrical and Computer Engineering. We are aiming to bring awareness of all that it offers to its students, faculty, and beloved alumni. Join us on April 12th for a day filled with fun workshops, company speakers, and free food!
