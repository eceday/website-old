  What is ECE Day?

ECE Day is a celebration of everything that encompasses the spirit of Electrical and Computer Engineering. We are aiming to bring awareness of all that it offers to its students, faculty, and beloved alumni. Join us on April 12th for a day filled with fun workshops, company speakers, and free food!

  Frequently Asked Questions
What time is ECE Day?
The day starts in the morning and activities extend through the evening.
Who can attend ECE Day?
Everyone who is interested in ECE is encouraged to attend! You do not have to be a current or future ECE student!
Will there be food available?
There will be free food trucks available for lunch and a keynote dinner in the evening.
How do I volunteer?
We will start reaching out for volunteers in March! Look out for a link then!
